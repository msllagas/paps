About Us

Welcome to our premier pet hotel and grooming facility, where we provide exceptional care and services for your beloved furry companions.

Our establishment is designed to cater to the needs of both dogs and cats, ensuring they receive the utmost comfort, attention, and pampering throughout their stay.

why us?

So... Why you should choose us?

Because we prioritize the well-being and happiness of your pets.

At our pet hotel, we offer spacious, clean, and comfortable accommodations, ensuring that each guest has a cozy place to rest and relax. Our rooms are thoughtfully designed to mimic a home- like environment, complete with comfortable bedding, toys, and soothing background music to create a calming atmosphere.

Our dedicated and highly trained staff members are passionate animal lovers who are committed to providing personalized care for each guest. They are experienced in handling various breeds and temperaments, ensuring your pet feels safe, secure, and loved during their stay. We prioritize individualized attention and ensure that all dietary and medical needs are met, including administering medication if required.

Whether you're heading out of town or simply need a day of pampering for your pet, our pet hotel and grooming facility is the perfect choice.

─ PAPS

Boarding Daycare Requirements

  1. Up-to-date vaccinations
    1. Rabies
    2. 5in1 OR 6in1 OR 8in1
    3. Bordetella (Kennel cough)
    4. Treatment for ticks & fleas

      5. All these vaccinations + treatment must be given to your pet AT LEAST 2 DAYS prior to Boarding or Daycare.

pet hotel

How long can my pet stay in a boarding facility?

The duration of your pet's stay depends on your specific needs. Our pet hotel usually offers various options, from short stays (a few days) to extended stays (weeks or even months).

Was this helpful?

Will my pet receive individual attention and exercise while boarding?

Yes, reputable boarding facilities provide individual attention, exercise, and playtime for pets. They often offer additional services, such as walks and interactive play, to keep your pet happy and active.

Was this helpful?

How does pet boarding work?

Our pet hotel has designated areas or kennels where pets can stay. Owners drop off their pets along with their belongings, and our staff takes care of their needs, including feeding, exercise, and sometimes additional services like grooming or playtime.

Was this helpful?

grooming

What services are typically included in pet grooming?

Our pet grooming services often include bathing, brushing, nail trimming, ear cleaning, fur trimming or styling, and sometimes additional services like teeth brushing.

Was this helpful?

Can we wait and watch our pet(s) being groomed at the facility?

Absolutely. We even offer free treats for your pets after grooming which is available at the counter.

Was this helpful?

Why is pet grooming important?

It is essential for maintaining a pet's overall health and well-being. It helps prevent matting, skin infections, and other skin-related issues.
